Alu Vorta - আলু ভর্তা

Alu vorta, is a dish made by mashing boiled soft potatoes and mixing chopped chilies, onion, salt and mustard oil. It is usually eaten as a side dish in West Bengal and Bangladesh

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Mustard Oil

Steps

  1. First you have to boil the potatoes in a separate pot
  2. Boiled potatoes should be crushed with the help of hands
  3. Mix well with fried onion, pepper and mustard oil. Proper amount of salt should be given
